Background

Over the years I tried to combine multiple interests such as graphic design, cinema and video games to work in a field that suits me. Thus, along the way I discovered the UX Design.

My first steps were in 2012, when I was in charge of my group project website. Since that time, I continued my ride with various projects such as a video game, websites about home services, city transport, but also communication agency, touristic guide. Moreover, I recently expanded my field of action with an email management app.

These projects allow me to practice and extend my knowledges and responsabilities in the aim to increase my open-mindedness and efficiency.
